What types of properties does Thanjai Curator specialize in?expand_more
We focus on premium residential estates, heritage villas, luxury farmhouses, and curated plotted developments in Thanjavur, Chennai, Coimbatore, and Madurai.
Do you offer assistance for NRI buyers?expand_more
Absolutely. Our dedicated NRI desk handles legal documentation, virtual tours, and end-to-end transaction management for overseas investors.
How do you verify exclusive owner properties?expand_more
We conduct rigorous title verification, site inspections, and legal due diligence. Every listing is cross-checked with original documents.
Can I list my property for sale with your platform?expand_more
Yes. We provide a bespoke listing service for owners, including professional photography, curated copywriting, and targeted marketing to qualified buyers.
What makes your real estate advisory different?expand_more
We combine deep local heritage knowledge with contemporary investment analytics — treating each property as a legacy asset, not just a transaction.
Do you provide legal assistance for property registration?expand_more
Yes, our legal partners assist with sale deed drafting, EC verification, and coordinating the registration process at the Sub-Registrar office.
Are there any hidden consultancy charges?expand_more
Transparency is our core value. All service fees are discussed upfront before the engagement begins; there are no hidden costs in our transactions.
Do you handle property management for vacant lands?expand_more
For our exclusive clients, we offer plot maintenance services including periodic inspections, fencing coordination, and tax payment management.
Can I get a virtual site visit if I am out of town?expand_more
Yes, we offer high-definition video walkthroughs and live guided virtual tours via Zoom or WhatsApp for all our featured properties.
What is the average timeline for closing a deal?expand_more
Depending on documentation and loan approvals, a standard transaction typically takes 15 to 45 days from the initial token advance.
